About All Risks, Ltd.

Independence in Actions

All Risks is the largest independent wholesale insurance broker in the country with $2B in premium and an annual growth rate of 18 percent.

All Risks, Ltd. was formed as an excess and surplus lines facility in 1964. Employing over 875 professionals in 28 locations, we deliver comprehensive commercial and personal insurance solutions exclusively to retail agents and brokers. As brokers or underwriters, we are our agents' licensed navigators in the specialty insurance market. All Risks provides a full-service platform of brokerage, contract binding and program capability.

Recognized by Business Insurance Journal as the top independent wholesale insurance broker in the country, our growth has surpassed our competitors for over 25 years; the result of an aggressive and entrepreneurial culture focused on providing new opportunities and superior customer service.

All Risks, Ltd. National Specialty Programs

The National Specialty Programs unit has been an underwriter and specialty insurance programs administrator for 20 years. What started as book of security guard business in 1992 has grown to over 35 niche business classes. There are in-house, specialty program underwriters in numerous All Risks offices, to quote, bind, issue and service policies and monitor rate adequacy on behalf of our carder partners.

With in-house binding authority, multi-line capability and including some of the broadest coverage available, our program developers seek niche markets with few viable specialty insurance programs. Contact our program spe-cialists today to learn more about our solutions.

All Risks National Specialty Programs unit provides:
  • Quoting & binding
  • Issue and service policies
  • Monitor rate adequacy
  • Place reinsurance
