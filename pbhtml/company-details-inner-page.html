    9 Important Coverages for Client's to Carry on Their Houseboat

    Back

    Posted on May 17, 2019 - Republish on May 17, 2019 by American Marine Insurance

    Like 17 Viewed 21

    As the #1 insurer of houseboats in the United States, American Marine Insurance provides agents with the ability to insure their clients who own houseboats. No matter whether they use their houseboat for timeshare rental, commercial rental, or vacations, American Marine covers all types of houseboat insurance coverage at highly competitive prices.

    Coverages Offered

    Coverages offered by American Marine for houseboats include:

    • Agreed Value for Hull and Equipment
    • Personal Effects
    • Medical Payments
    • Liability Coverage for Bodily Injury and Property Damage
    • Sports Liability Coverage
    • Pollution Liability Coverage
    • Uninsured Boater Coverage
    • Emergency Towing and Assistance
    • Automatic Coverage for Tenders

    Don't let clients end up with too little coverage. Ensure that they completely understand the need for all 9 coverage options.

    Difficulty Finding Coverage

    Including houseboat insurance for client's seeking marine insurance is a must. When a client comes to you and asks about marine insurance coverage, don't forget to include the houseboat!

    Many clients report having trouble finding adequate coverage for their houseboat. American Marine Insurance can help your business become the "go to" agency for houseboat insurance coverage.

    Location

    Depending on where the houseboat is located, different conversations pertaining to hurricanes, freezing waters, or other natural events will help agents determine the best type of houseboat insurance to provide for their clients. Working with an expert insurance partner, like American Marine Insurance, can help agents provide their custom-ers with the best houseboat insurance package that fits their exact needs.

    American Marine Insurance

    Creating a sound partnership with a trusted carrier is one of an agent's best resources. Working only with known An rated carriers, American Marine Insurance can help agents differentiate themselves and their agency to become an industry leader in providing expert marine insurance coverage.

    Founded by marine experts, American Marine Insurance has stayed true to our roots by offering marine insurance only for commercial and personal use anywhere in the world. With full-time use and moorage in the United States, Canada, Mexico, and Costa Rica, American Marine is the perfect partner for agents who want to find the perfect inland marine coverage for their customers.

    Specializing in all aspects of marine insurance coverage, American Marine's insurance products include:
    • Competitive prices
    • Excellent claims service
    • Flexible payment options
    • Tailored coverage
    • Valuable options

    Make growing your business simpler by honing your talents and focusing your efforts on rapport building and long-term relationships with reputable carriers, wholesalers, and organizations.

    Carrying knowledge in the marine insurance niche sector is powerful. Stay up to date on local and state laws. If selling insurance in various states, remember to remain compliant with programs and certificate renewals. Quickly, you will see how all of these efforts can lead to your business growing.
